Mess Directory Case Study

Problem: Many students living in hostels or renting rooms near their institutions face difficulties cooking regularly, while daily food ordering becomes expensive.

Solution: Built a dedicated Mess Directory web page as a centralized platform to discover nearby mess options with complete decision-making information.

Core Features:

  • Searchable list of nearby messes with location details and ratings.
  • Daily or weekly menu visibility with transparent pricing and package options.
  • Student review and rating system for better trust and comparison.
  • Meal plan/package information for different budgets from breakfast to dinner.
  • Contact details and directions for easy access to mess operators.

Impact: Helps students find affordable and convenient meal options quickly while also helping local messes attract more customers and build a stronger local community.
